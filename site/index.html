Offline screen capture for Mac.

ScreenshotMaxxing is an offline Mac screen capture utility. Capture screenshots, record videos, redact, annotate, copy, and save without accounts, subscriptions, usage tracking, or cloud sync.

No accounts. No subscription. No usage tracking. No cloud sync.

Captures and edits stay local unless you choose to share or export them. The app is built as an offline Mac utility, not a service.

  • No sign in
  • No payment flow
  • No telemetry
  • No hosted screenshot library

Focused capture workflow

Small, local-first tools for the parts of capture work that should stay fast.

Menu bar capture

Keep common screenshot and recording actions close without turning capture into a full document workflow.

Redaction before export

Blur or pixelate sensitive areas so exported pixels do not carry private information forward.

Local history

Store screenshots and recordings on your Mac with local metadata, instead of sending a library to a hosted account.

Copy and save

Move edited screenshots into the next app quickly, or save files when you need a durable copy.
